#################
Community Support
#################

.. This section should provide a brief, top-level description of the page.

The process of designing the LSST survey strategy has been a partnership between the project
and the community. This work is guided by the community representatives on the :ref:`SCOC <SCOC-Index>`.

To help understand effects of the survey strategy, the project has provided simulations of the 10-year survey and a framework for contributing metrics measuring survey strategy "success".
